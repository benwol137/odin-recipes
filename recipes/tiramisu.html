Tiramisu

Descritption

Here is Authentic Italian Tiramisu Recipe, an Italian dessert made of savoiardi ladyfingers soaked in coffee,

arranged in layers and filled with a cream made with mascarpone and eggs.

Ingredients

  • 300 g (about 30) Savoiardi Ladyfingers
  • 500 g (1,1 lb = 2 1/4 cups) of mascarpone cheese
  • 4 medium eggs (about 220 g/7,7 oz without shells)
  • 100 g (1/2 cup) of granulated sugar
  • 300 ml (1 Â¼ cup) of coffee (better if espresso)
  • 2 tablespoons of Rum or Marsala
  • unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps

  1. First of all, make the coffee
  2. Separate the egg whites from the yolks
  3. Now, in a bowl, beat the egg yolks with sugar
  4. In the meantime, pour the mascarpone cheese
  5. Using the flexible-edge k-beater
  6. Mix with a wooden spoon
